About

Professional voice and speech care rooted in the community it serves.

Trans artist — artwork coming soon

I'm a speech-language pathologist who works with trans and gender-diverse clients on voice—and with kids and families on outpatient speech needs. This practice exists because I wanted care that is evidence-based, collaborative, and grounded in who we actually are.

My approach is not about drilling you into a voice or overwhelming you with jargon. We focus on your goals, your comfort, and vocal health—whether that's pitch, resonance, communication, or pediatric speech support.

This practice runs on donations and mutual aid because I believe care should be accessible. We highlight trans artists and community instead of centering a personal brand.
