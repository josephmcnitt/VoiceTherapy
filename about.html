About

Trans-owned. Trans-run. For trans people.

I’m a voice therapist who works with trans and gender-diverse clients. This practice exists because I wanted care that was professional, evidence-based, and rooted in the community it serves.

I’ve trained in voice and work with clients one-on-one. My approach is collaborative: we focus on your goals, your comfort, and what you want your voice to do for you—whether that’s pitch, resonance, communication, or something else.

This practice runs on donations and mutual aid because I believe voice care should be accessible. If that resonates with you, I’d be glad to hear from you.
